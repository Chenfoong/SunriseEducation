Our Model

Sunrise Homeschooling

Sunrise Homeschooling is Malaysia's leading Cambridge-aligned homeschool programme, offering families the freedom of personalised education without compromising academic excellence. We combine structured IGCSE curricula with flexible, home-centred learning philosophies that honour each child's unique pace and style.

Students benefit from small class sizes (maximum 15 per class), individualised attention, bilingual instruction in English and Mandarin, and a vibrant community of like-minded families who value education, character, and holistic development above all. Our approach has proven successful for over 15 years, with graduates now studying at top universities worldwide.

🎯 Why Families Choose Us: Personalised learning paths, flexible scheduling for family life, internationally recognised Cambridge certification, and a warm community that feels like family.

Cambridge IGCSE Syllabus

The Cambridge International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) is the world's most popular international qualification for 14 to 16-year-olds, developed by Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE), UK. It is recognised by leading universities and employers in over 160 countries.

At Sunrise Education, we deliver the complete Cambridge IGCSE programme with certified, experienced educators, comprehensive study materials, and a supportive bilingual academic environment tailored for Malaysian homeschooling families. Our students consistently achieve results above the global average.

🎓 Cambridge Certification: All IGCSE results are issued directly by CAIE, UK — ensuring full international recognition for university applications worldwide, including IPTA and IPTS in Malaysia.

Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, email, or visit our campus. Our friendly admissions team will explain all available programmes, answer your questions, and provide an information pack.

Schedule a personalised campus tour to meet our teachers, explore our facilities, sit in on a class, and experience the warm Sunrise learning environment firsthand.

Receive your orientation pack, timetable, and welcome kit. Your Sunrise Education journey officially begins — we are honoured to have your family join us!

About Sunrise Education

Our Story 我们的故事

Sunrise Education was born from a single, powerful belief: every child, regardless of background, deserves access to world-class education delivered with warmth, integrity, and deep personal care. Founded over 15 years ago in Klang, Selangor, we began as a humble homeschooling resource centre for Malaysian families seeking a credible, nurturing alternative to conventional schooling.

What started as a small community of dedicated educators and enthusiastic families has grown into a thriving institution with two campuses and over 500 students — all while maintaining the close-knit, family-centred atmosphere that makes Sunrise special. We've stayed true to our founding principle: education works best when it's personal.

Today, Sunrise Education is proud to be a recognised Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) centre, offering the prestigious IGCSE programme in a bilingual, culturally rich, and deeply nurturing environment that feels less like an institution and more like a second home.
