Our Model

Sunrise Homeschooling

Sunrise Homeschooling is Malaysia's leading Cambridge-aligned homeschool programme, offering families the freedom of personalised education without compromising academic excellence. We combine structured IGCSE curricula with flexible, home-centred learning philosophies.

Students benefit from small class sizes, individualised attention, bilingual instruction in English and Mandarin, and a vibrant community of like-minded families who value education, values, and character formation above all.

Our Missions

🎯 Academic Excellence+
To provide a world-class Cambridge IGCSE education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and credentials recognised by leading universities globally. We uphold the highest academic standards while making quality education accessible to every family.
💡 Inspire Lifelong Curiosity+
To ignite a genuine love of learning in every student — nurturing intellectual curiosity, creative thinking, and a growth mindset that will serve them throughout their entire life journey, not just through examinations.
🌍 Build Global Citizens+
To develop culturally aware, ethically grounded, and globally minded individuals who can contribute positively to their communities, their nation of Malaysia, and the world at large — as compassionate, principled leaders.
❤️ Holistic Character Formation+
To cultivate strong moral character, emotional resilience, social empathy, and a sense of personal responsibility in every student — because excellent grades without good character do not make a complete person.
👨‍👩‍👧 Empower Families+
To be a trusted partner for families who value personalised, values-driven education. We believe parents are irreplaceable in the educational process and work hand-in-hand with them to shape their children's futures.
🏫 Community Building+
To foster a warm, inclusive, and vibrant community of learners, educators, and families who support one another — creating a network of relationships that enrich lives far beyond the classroom walls.

Cambridge IGCSE Syllabus

The Cambridge International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) is the world's most popular international qualification for 14 to 16-year-olds. Developed by Cambridge Assessment International Education, it is recognised by leading universities and employers worldwide.

At Sunrise Education, we deliver the complete Cambridge IGCSE programme with certified, experienced educators, comprehensive study materials, and a supportive academic environment tailored for Malaysian homeschooling families.

🎓 Cambridge Certification

All our IGCSE results are issued directly by Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE), UK — ensuring full international recognition and credibility for university applications worldwide.

About Sunrise Education

Our Story 我们的故事

Sunrise Education was born from a single, powerful belief: that every child, regardless of background, deserves access to world-class education delivered with warmth, integrity, and deep personal care.

Founded over 15 years ago in Klang, Selangor, we began as a humble homeschooling resource centre for Malaysian families seeking a credible alternative to conventional schooling. What started as a small community of dedicated educators and enthusiastic families has grown into a thriving institution with two campuses and over 500 students.

Today, Sunrise Education is proud to be a recognised Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) centre — offering the prestigious IGCSE programme in a bilingual, culturally rich, and deeply nurturing environment that feels less like a school and more like a second home.
